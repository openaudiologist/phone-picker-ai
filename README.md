# PhonePicker AI

PhonePicker AI helps Indian buyers find the right smartphone without the hassle of endless research. You answer five quick questions — your budget, what you mainly use your phone for, whether you have a brand preference, what phone you currently own, and any must-have features like 5G or fast charging — and the AI does the rest. In seconds, it returns the top 3 phones matched specifically to your answers, each with a plain-English explanation of why it suits you, a full spec breakdown, scored ratings across camera, battery, performance, display, and value, and a direct link to buy it on Amazon India.

## Features

- **5-step guided form** — walks you through budget, use case, brand, current phone, and must-have features at your own pace. Every step is skippable or adjustable before you submit.
- **AI-powered recommendations** — a large language model analyses your answers and picks the 3 best-fit phones from the current Indian market, ranked by how well they match your needs.
- **Personalised explanations** — each phone comes with a 2–3 sentence "why this phone is for you" written specifically for your use case, not a generic product description.
- **Specs & scores** — display, processor, camera, battery, RAM, and OS are listed for every phone alongside visual score bars (0–100) for the metrics that matter most to buyers.
- **Pros & cons** — concise bullet points highlighting each phone's strengths and trade-offs so you can compare at a glance.
- **Buy on Amazon India** — one click takes you to the phone's Amazon India listing so you can check live pricing, reviews, and purchase immediately.
- **No sign-up, no data stored** — the tool is completely free to use, requires no account, and does not save any of your answers.
